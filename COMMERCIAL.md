## What a commercial license can grant

The commercial license is negotiated per engagement and is not a single off-the-shelf document, but the scope typically covers some combination of:

- The right to run a modified instance of ArtifactFlow over a network without the AGPL Section 13 corresponding-source obligation.

- The right to integrate ArtifactFlow, or substantial parts of it, into a closed commercial product or service.

Terms such as price, warranties, support, jurisdiction, audit, and termination are negotiated in writing. There is no implicit grant from the AGPL release. The commercial license must be in writing and signed by the project owner before any non-AGPL use begins.
